# Scoutpost pricing

Hosted Scoutpost uses monthly credits.

## Plans

- **Free**: $0/month, 100 credits
- **Pro**: $10/month, 1,000 credits
- **Team**: $50/month, 5,000 shared credits

## Typical run costs

- **Page Scout**: 1 credit
- **Beat Scout**: 7 credits
- **Social Scout**: 2 credits for Instagram, X, TikTok, and LinkedIn; 15 for Facebook
- **Civic Scout**: 10 credits in the current public docs cost table

Always disclose spend before triggering expensive runs, especially Civic Scouts or batches of many scouts.
